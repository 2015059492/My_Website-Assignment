About us

Sithandani foundation was born out of the wish of Thembi to put Dr Moshal's words into action: Lets Pay It Forward.

We are: A Registered non-profit and public benefit organisation located in the King Cetshwayo district in Melmoth, KwaZulu-Natal.
We build partnerships and work collaboratively with the public and private sector as well as local organisations in order to serve those:
living in KwaMagwaza, EThengele, Bomvini, Thubalethu, Thunzini and Mfanefile who have been infected/affected by:
HIV/AIDS and TB as well as widows, orphans and the destitute.

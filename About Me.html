Thembisile Buthelezi

Thembisile Buthelezi is a young lady of age 22, born and birthed in a small village called EThengela, Melmoth town in KwaZulu Natal. She did her primary and secondary school there and furthered her tertiary studies at UFS, Bloemfontein Campus. She is a founder of TheRoseBeauty beauty bar. She is a content creator, specialising in fitness and make-up. She is also a Bachelor of Science graduate specialising in Microbiology and Genetics, from the University of the Free State. She believes in Education, Women Empowerment and Independence as well as bettering lives of our communities.
